The majority of the work I do focuses on Interaction Design (web, mobile, UX/UI Design, etc.) and Branding partnering with startups and agencies on their digital experience needs. My process includes researching through competitive audits, business trends, and case studies with user stories; designing sketches and prototypes (wireframes, mockups, demos); and developing front-end and CMS solutions (WordPress, Squarespace, & Shopify).

Feel free to contact me if you are working on an interesting project.
